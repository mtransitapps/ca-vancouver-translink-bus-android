Full description:
This app adds Vancouver TransLink buses information to MonTransit BETA.

This app provides the buses schedule and news from www.translink.ca, YouTube and @TransLink and @TransLinkMedia on Twitter.

BETA: Thanks for your patience while the last features are being added and the last bugs are being fixed.

TransLink buses serves Vancouver in British Columbia, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit BETA app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit BETA app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by TransLink.
http://www.translink.ca/en/Schedules-and-Maps/Developer-Resources.aspx

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-vancouver-translink-bus-android

This app is not related with TransLink (South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority) 
and BCRTC (British Columbia Rapid Transit Company).
